Timeline

November 2014

Founder & CEO Dale Mabilangan meets his first High School friend and later on long time partner Jeffrey Daniels.

March 2017

After being bullied in High School for not having enough money to purchase their own trending clothing to fit with all the other kids, Dale & Jeffrey come up with an idea to build their own brand called "Obligated".

July 2018

After a year of saving up enough money Dale & Jeffrey finally completed their goal of saving enough money to license their company, "Obligated".

September 2018

Obligated brand was formed.

November 2018

Dale & Jeffrey patented a new material that can be used to produce high end trendy clothing while being environmentally friendly and stylish at the same time.

December 2019

After a year has passed, Obligated company has become well-known through out the world, Making record number sales and competing with other hype beast fashion brands for the top spot in the world of fashion.
